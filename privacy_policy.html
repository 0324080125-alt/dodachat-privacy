DodaChat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DodaChat. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our messaging application and services.

By using DodaChat, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Data

  • Phone number (verified via SMS)
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted using bcrypt)

Messages & Media

  • Text messages you send
  • Images and videos you share
  • Stories you post (visible for 24 hours)
  • Comments and likes on stories

Device & Session Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • IP address (for security)
  • Session data

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide one-on-one text messaging
  • Enable image and video sharing
  • Display stories for 24 hours
  • Verify your phone number via Twilio SMS
  • Deliver messages between users
  • Send push notifications for new messages
  • Send password reset codes via SMS
  • Manage active sessions across devices
  • Detect suspicious login attempts
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Prevent fraud and ensure account security

4. Data Security

  • Passwords encrypted using bcrypt hashing algorithm
  • Phone verification via Twilio Verify API
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Messages stored securely on encrypted servers
  • Regular security updates

Note: End-to-end encryption is not currently implemented but planned for future updates.

5. Information Sharing

We DO NOT Sell Your Data

Your personal information is never sold to third parties.

Service Providers

We share data with:

  • Twilio (SMS verification)
  • Render (backend hosting)
  • Railway (database)
  • Firebase (push notifications)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or court order.

6. Data Storage & Retention

  • Backend hosted on Render cloud platform
  • Database stored on Railway cloud servers
  • Messages retained until you delete them
  • Stories automatically delete after 24 hours
  • Session data kept for active logins only
  • Account data kept while your account is active
  • Deleted messages removed from our servers
  • Account deletion removes all associated data

7. Your Rights

  • Change your password anytime
  • Delete individual messages you sent
  • Block users to prevent them from contacting you
  • Delete your account and all associated data

8. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: DodaChat is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Parental Notice: If we become aware of data collection from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact us at b76561134@gmail.com

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be notified via in-app notification. Please review this policy periodically for updates.
