DodaChat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DodaChat. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our messaging application.

By using DodaChat, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Phone Number: Used for account verification via Twilio SMS
  • Username: Your chosen display name
  • Password: Encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • Profile Picture: Optional photo you upload

2.2 Content You Share

  • Messages: Text messages you send to other users
  • Photos and Videos: Media you share in chats
  • Stories: Photos and videos you post as 24-hour stories
  • Story Interactions: Likes and comments you make on stories

2.3 Usage Information (Stored Locally on Your Device)

  • Login Session: Session token and login timestamp
  • App Preferences: Theme mode, notification settings
  • Usage Statistics: App launch count, stories created count
  • Viewed Stories: List of stories you've viewed (for offline tracking)
  • Story Drafts: Unsent stories saved temporarily
Note: We do NOT collect IP addresses, device identifiers, or location data. Usage information listed above is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to our servers.

3. Permissions We Request

  • Photos/Storage: To let you select photos and videos for stories and profile pictures
  • Camera: To take photos directly within the app
  • Notifications: To alert you about new messages and story activity

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your phone number via Twilio SMS
  • Enable one-on-one messaging between users
  • Display and share your stories for 24 hours
  • Send push notifications for new messages (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Send password reset codes via SMS
  • Provide story likes and comments functionality
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

5. Data Storage & Retention

5.1 Server Storage

  • Backend: Hosted on Render cloud platform
  • Database: MySQL database on Railway cloud servers
  • Messages: Retained until you delete them
  • Stories: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Account Data: Kept while your account is active
  • Deleted Content: Removed from our servers immediately

5.2 Local Device Storage

  • Session data (login token, timestamp)
  • App preferences (theme, notifications)
  • Viewed stories tracking
  • Story drafts (until published or cleared)
  • Usage statistics (launch count, etc.)

All local data is cleared when you log out or uninstall the app.

6. Information Sharing

6.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

Your personal information is never sold to advertisers or third parties.

6.2 Service Providers We Use

  • Twilio: SMS verification for phone numbers
  • Render: Backend hosting
  • Railway: Database hosting
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Google AdMob: Displays ads in the app (AdMob may collect device info for ad targeting)

6.3 With Other Users

When you use DodaChat, your messages and stories are shared with the recipients you choose. Your profile picture and username are visible to users you chat with.

6.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights and user safety.

7. Data Security

  • All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing (never stored in plain text)
  • Phone verification via Twilio's secure Verify API
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Messages and media stored securely on encrypted servers
  • Regular security updates to protect against vulnerabilities
Important: End-to-end encryption (where only sender and receiver can read messages) is not currently implemented but is planned for future updates.

8. Your Rights & Controls

  • Change your password anytime
  • Update your profile picture
  • Delete individual messages you sent
  • Delete your own stories
  • Block users to prevent them from contacting you
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently

9. Advertising

DodaChat displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device information for ad personalization according to Google's privacy policy. You can learn more about Google's ad practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

10. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: DodaChat is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Parental Notice: If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact us at b76561134@gmail.com

11. International Users

DodaChat is available worldwide. Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers in different countries. By using DodaChat, you consent to these data transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or app features. When we make material changes, we will notify you via:

  • In-app notification
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. Your Consent

By using DodaChat, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use DodaChat.
