DodaChat Account Deletion

Delete Your Account In-App

You can delete your DodaChat account directly from the app:

  1. Open the DodaChat app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm with your password
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your messages, stories, and data will be permanently deleted.
